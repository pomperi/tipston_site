FAQ

 Is there online multiplayer?

Online multiplayer is available through Parsec and Steam Remote Play Together. While native online multiplayer would be an incredible feature to have, it's unfortunately too big of an undertaking for a single developer on a project of this size. Sorry!


 Can I play alone?

Tipston Salvage is technically a co-op only game. There is however a smaller bonus campaign available, made specifically for solo play. It's mainly there as an extra challenge or for trying out the game before getting a group together to play


 Will it be released on console?

The initial release will be on PC only. The ambition is to get the game out on console at a later stage. It is a game that is best suited to be played on a couch in front of a TV after all!


 When is the game coming out?

The game is coming out on Steam in 2024.
There is no exact date yet, but the development is wrapping up and a final release date is coming soon! In the meantime there is a playable demo on Steam that you can try out :)
